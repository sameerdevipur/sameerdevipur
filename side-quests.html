Assassin’s Creed Series

Historical reconstruction · Parkour systems · Iterative design · Worldbuilding

A long-running experiment in blending historical fiction with interactive traversal. At its best, the series treated architecture as a learning environment and ideology as narrative engine.

The early entries framed the tension between free will and order with unusual clarity. Later titles shifted toward scale and spectacle, sometimes at the cost of narrative tightness.

Mechanic worth stealing: Use environment as curriculum. Let architecture teach the player.
