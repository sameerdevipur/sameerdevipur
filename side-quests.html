Side quests

This page is a loose collection of games I have played, movies and shows that stuck with me, and a growing pile of miscellaneous trivia that did not fit anywhere else.

Nothing here is ranked or curated too carefully. It is simply a snapshot of what I enjoy, what I revisit, and the small distractions that quietly shape how I think and unwind.

Games That Changed My Thinking

Not favorites. Systems that altered how I think about structure, strategy, and consequence.

Outer Wilds

Exploration · Knowledge progression · Systems design

Progress is not tied to power or inventory. It is tied to understanding. Information itself becomes the unlock mechanism.

Mechanic worth stealing: Insight should be the reward, not accumulation.

The Legend of Zelda Series

Spatial reasoning · Iterative mastery

Simple mechanics introduced early are recombined into complex problem spaces. Constraint drives creativity.

Mechanic worth stealing: Build depth through recombination, not complexity.
