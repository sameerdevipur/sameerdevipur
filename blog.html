I used to write short articles and fun stories on thedopplerdude.blogspot.com , which started as a team writing experiment with a couple of friends and slowly turned into a place for ideas that did not quite fit anywhere else.

At the moment, this page is in a quiet phase, thanks to me going through a writer’s block. The intention, however, is still very much alive.

Someday this space will fill up again with unfiltered thoughts, half-formed ideas, and observations that feel worth writing down, even if they are not fully polished.
