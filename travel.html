Travel diary

This page is a collection of trips, short detours, and snapshots taken along the way. Some are from planned travel, others from moments that simply felt worth capturing.

Together, they form a quiet record of places I have passed through and the way they looked to me at the time. No guides, no reviews, just fragments of movement and observation.
